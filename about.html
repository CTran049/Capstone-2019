About Us

This two-year interactive program teaches students the latest techniques in hairstyling, haircutting, and coloring from experienced cosmetology professionals in a state-of-the-art salon setting. West-MEC programs prepare students to seek one or more industry certification or licensure in their field of study, providing a pathway to economic independence.
